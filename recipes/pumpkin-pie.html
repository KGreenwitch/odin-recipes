Pumpkin Pie

A vegetable for desert? Nevermind, lets make that dinner instead

When I was growing up my mom would make desert for dinner. My favorite was pumpkin pie night, when everyone got half a pie for dinner. Back then my favorite part was all the Koolwhip that I would cover my half of pie in.

This recipe is very differant than the one I grew up with. There is no gluten, dairy, or refined sugars here. So now I get to enjoy eating the pie and not be sick afterwords.

Ingredients

  • 1 homemade gluten free pie crust Here is a link to a great one
  • 15oz can pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup full fat coconut milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 t vanilla extract
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 4 t cinnamon
  • 2 t ginger
  • 1 t cloves
  • 1/4 t nutmeg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Put everything into blender and blend well
  3. Pour filling into pie crust
  4. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, until very middle no longer jiggels
  5. Remove from oven and let cool for at least 30 minutes
