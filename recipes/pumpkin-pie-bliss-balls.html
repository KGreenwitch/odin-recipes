Pumpkin pie bliss balls

Get your daily pumpkin pie fix with this protein packed treat

These balls are a perfect little snack in the middle of the day.
My favorite brand of collagen is from Perfect Supplements
I find that the dates blend better if you soak them in hot water for atleast 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 10 dates, softened in hot water
  • 1 cup raw unsalted pecans
  • 2 T pumpkin puree
  • 2 scoops collagen
  • 1/4 cup tigernut flour
  • 1 t cinnamon
  • 1/2 t ginger
  • 1/8 t cloves

Optional ingredients to roll the balls in

  • shredded coconut
  • cacao powder
  • more cinnamon

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients into food processor and process until dough forms.
  2. Form into balls and roll in chosen toppings.
  3. Put in freezer to stiffen up before eating.
