Gluten Free Pie Crust

You can't make pie without a crust

Sure, technically you can make a crustless pie, but that is sad. This crust works well for pumpkin pie and makes for a delicious quiche

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups tapioca flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 1 T gelatin
  • 3/4 cup + 2 T palm shortning
  • 1 egg
  • 2 T cold water
  • 1 t apple cider vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flours, salt, and gelatin in a bowl
  3. Cut shortening into flour mixure.
  4. Add egg, water, and viegar and mix with hands until well combined
  5. Roll dough between two pieces of parchment paper until it is as large as the pie pan. Remove top piece of paper, put pan upside down on top of the dough and carefully flip over and gently press dough into pan.
  6. Trim any excess dough and shape edges. Poke holes in bottom of crust with a fork.
  7. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes
